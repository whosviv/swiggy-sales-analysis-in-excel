# Swiggy Sales Analysis Dashboard — Excel | Data Analytics Project

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** created using **Microsoft Excel**, designed to analyze Swiggy’s sales performance across Indian cities.
The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, customer behavior, food category performance, and state-wise sales distribution using **Pivot Tables, Power Pivot, Charts, and Slicers**.

---

## Key Features

### KPI Summary

Displays the top business metrics:

* **Total Sales:** ₹53.01M
* **Avg Rating:** 4.34
* **Rating Count:** 5.59M
* **Total Orders:** 197.43K
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹268.51

---

### Monthly Sales Trend

* Line chart showing month-over-month revenue changes
* Helps identify peaks (e.g., May & Aug) and dips (Feb)

---

### Daily & Weekly Sales Trends

* Bar chart showing sales by day of the week
* Weekly trend chart identifying high-performing weeks

---

### Sales by Food Type

* Donut chart showing contribution of **Veg vs Non-Veg** dishes
* Non-Veg: ₹18.8M
* Veg: ₹34.2M

---

### Sales by State (Map Chart)

* Interactive India map highlighting revenue distribution
* Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Delhi among top contributors

---

### Top 5 Cities by Sales

* Horizontal bar chart ranking the highest-revenue cities
* Bengaluru ranks #1 with ₹5.46M

---

## Dashboard Insights**

* Saturday has the **highest sales** (₹7.78M)
* Veg dishes contribute **64% of total revenue**
* May shows the **strongest monthly performance**
* Bengaluru leads top cities with **₹5.46M** in sales
* Q2 performs slightly better than Q1 and Q3
